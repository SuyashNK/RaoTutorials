Rao Tutorials
Here the Success follows the Dreams

The Rao Tutorials is the coaching institute for the students from Standard 5th to the 12th Standard of Science stream. This coaching institute helps the students to improve themselves and our well educated and well experienced faculties will help them to archeive what is beyond their exceptions.

Education is most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world

Our History

We started our very own first batch in 2014. And we are giving a very good amount of scholar students to the Topper's list of every School.

We try to unlock the hard working part of every student since 2014 and from all those 8 years we tried to develop the x factor of the student to unleash what is within them.

Vision & mission

We want to educate and make a student fulfilled for his/her further steps of life with proper guiding and orientation.

We want to clear each every concept and doubt of our student to make him/her ready to face the Exams.

Our procedure

Learn

In this phase, we teach the students the whole curriculum and solve their doubts as soon as possible. Our well educated and well experienced facuties provide the massive understandable experience for the students.

Practice

In This phase, we gave a proper oriented practice sessions for the students to make their way to the perfection. As we all know, Practice makes a man perfect.

Test

The Test phase is the phase where the students face the weekly examinations and test themselves, what they have learned and practiced during the above two phases. Apart from weekly tests, we also provides a couple of sets of Unit, Semester and Final examination, they will make them prepared for what they are going to face in school/institute.
